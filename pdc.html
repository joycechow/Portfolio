14th Participatory Design Conference in Aarhus, Denmark

As part of my work at the Interactive Institute Swedish ICT, I will have the honor of running an interactive exhibition workshop at the Participatory Design Conference in Aarhus, Denmark on August 17, 2016

Exploring challenges facing design practitioners

The theme of the conference is ”PARTICIPATORY DESIGN IN AN ERA OF PARTICIPATION”. The workshop I will run explores how to use cards to facilitate conversations in multidisciplinary teams. By inviting stakeholders to contribute by co-creating cards, we enable all views to be better heard and incorporated in decision making.

My role

These methods were developed through our work in a project that explores future ideas for math education in Swedish middle schools. My role in this project was in co-writing the project proposal which was accepted for funding by Vinnova, background research, user observations and interviews of students and teachers, planning design workshops, and concept development. I took a leading role in writing our accepted case study for publication (download available shortly), as well as planning and running the interactive exhibition at the Participatory Design Conference.
